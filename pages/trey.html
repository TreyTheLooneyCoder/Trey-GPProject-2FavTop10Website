#10 - Spiderman & Friends: Secret Missions (v.smile)


This top ten list is ordered based off of the way I feel about the games in this specific moment. This game is in my top ten because it was one of the first games I played ever, got it at a very young age before we moved to East Palo Alto and it was before I knew I was a gamer as well as before I had electronics, so I played it all the time. There are a lot of games I enjoy a lot more gameplay and story wise, but the nostalgic memory of playing this as a kid is what got this put on the list.

#7 - Spiderman ps4


This game is really fun I would still go back to play it today. It's really funny because this game was the game that made me start choosing the hardest difficulty in every game I play, because when I was starting I was about to pick casual and just enjoy a decent challenge and the story but my friend was trying to make fun of me because he played on a harder difficulty, so i put it on the hardest and struggled to beat the game as fast as possible so I could say I still finished first.

#6 - Yakuza


One of the best game series I've ever played, period. It's fast paced, has a good story, and makes you feel like the most aggressive and dangerous person alive. The entire game is getting attacked for poor reasons and showing people why they really should choose their targets wiser. The story of each game is different in it's own right, yet each one links up back to every game prior in some way and connects, they make a great use of throwing wacky and comedic side quest and things to do when bored with nothing to do. The character development for the main cast is insane, the way they introduce new characters and even new playable characters always is done perfectly. New characters always connect to the main plot of all games in some way or another and the best thing in my opinion is al the kinds of fighting and playing styles you can use over the course of the series, theres all kind of special finishes or "heat actions" to be specific with most new fighting styles breeding their own tree of new ones as well. It really disappointed me what they did with like a dragon but at least 2 of the spin offs were really good.
